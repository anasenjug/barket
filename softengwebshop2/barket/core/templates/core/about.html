About Us

Welcome to Barket, your online destination for all things pets!

Our Mission

Barket was founded with a clear mission - to provide pets and their owners with the highest quality products along with top-notch service.
Our passion for pets inspires us to constantly seek the latest and most innovative products
on the market to ensure the happiness and well-being of every member of your family.
